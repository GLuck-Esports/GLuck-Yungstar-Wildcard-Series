### GLuck Yungstar Series 1 – Additional Information

---
## Tournament Operator

- **Tournament Operator:** GLuck Esports

## Tournament Format

- **GLuck Yungstar Series 1:**

  - Online Open Qualifiers: Bo1 Single Elimination with unlimited sign ups, 3 teams qualifies to Closed Qualifier.
  - Online Open Qualifier Dates: 9th, 10th, 11th, 12th April 2026

  - Online Closed Qualifier: 12 qualified teams from 4 Open Qualifiers + 4 VRS invited teams, 4 GSL Groups Bo3. 8 teams qualifies to Main Event 
  - Online Closed Qualifier Dates: 24th - 26th April 2026
  
  - LAN Main Event: 8 qualified teams from Closed Qualifier, Double Elimination Bo3
  - Top 2 teams of Main Event qualifies to Wildcard LAN 
  - LAN Main Event: 22nd - 23rd May 2026
  - Main Event Prize Pool: $5.000

 _Open Qualifier slots can be increased if the hosting platform allows it_



## VRS Information
- **Tournament Tier** Tier 2
- **Ranked Stages:** **HLTV.org**, after our formal application, will determine which stages and matches will count toward VRS. Ranking status is not guaranteed until confirmed 
- **VRS list used:** Filtered ranking by countries: Russia, Belarus, Kazakhstan, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, and Georgia
- **VRS publication date used for ranking:** April 2026  
- **VRS publication date used for seeding:** April 2026
